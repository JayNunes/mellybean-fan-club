CHARACTERS IN THE MELLYBEAN SERIES...

Click to flip!

Melly

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Licking noses, playing tag, fetch, catch, racing and adventure!

Fun Facts: Melly is a scruffy Poodle mix. She is brave, curious and friendly.

Butternut

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Napping, chewing TV cables and climbing.

Fun Facts: Butternut is an orange tabby cat with ear tufts like a lynx. He's a bit of scaredy-cat. He is the oldest and the smallest of the cats.

Tugs

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Napping, eating and getting up on the kitchen counter.

Fun Facts: Tugs is a white and orange tabby cat. He is the biggest of the cats, and always thinking about food.

Chuck

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Napping, reading and scratching the furniture.

Fun Facts: Chuck is a medium sized orange tabby cat. He is great at coming up with a plan whenever the gang is problem solving.

Narra

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Helping people and hanging out with friends.

Fun Facts: Narra is a GIANT "Bunnycorn" who controls the magic element of "Life". He can heal plants and animals but he can also be FIERCE!!

Wilma

Appears in: #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Power, ancient magic secrets and luxury.

Fun Facts: Wilma is a wizard who's a lot older than she looks but uses magic to stay young. She is on an evil quest to steal all 4 ancient power elements.

The King

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Gold, bragging and cheating.

Fun Facts: The king is obsessed with gold, and will do terrible things to get it. He loves a good challenge if he wins but punishes people when he loses.

Ms. Cooper

Appears in: #1 The Giant Monster, #2 The Wicked Wizard.

Likes: Helping kids, sharing and running the orphanage.

Fun Facts: Ms. Cooper was arrested by the king for not paying her taxes. He chained her up in a dungeon where cakes and desserts were "just out of reach".

Hetty

Appears in: #2 The Wicked Wizard, #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Cheesy buns, visiting friends and adventure.

Fun Facts: Hetty is a "Hippocorn" who controls the magic element of "Air". She is the smallest of the magical ancient creatures, but she's a big part of the team.

Retta

Appears in: #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: Hiding in the North Sea and staying out of "human troubles".

Fun Facts: Retta is a "Dragonseal" who controls the magic element of "Water". She can fly, swim and create giant tidal waves with a flick of her tail.

Lemmy

Appears in: #3 The Villains' Revenge.

Likes: The "old days", when humans and creatures lived in harmony.

Fun Facts: Lemmy is a "Griffinbear" who controls the magic "Earth" element. He can move mountains with his magic and destroy armies with his strength.
